Overcoming Exam Anxiety

Exam stress is normal, but you can manage it. Start by creating a consistent study schedule and break your syllabus into manageable chunks. Practice mindfulness or deep breathing when you feel overwhelmed. Avoid caffeine and get proper sleep before exams. Most importantly, focus on progress, not perfection.
